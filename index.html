Welcome to All Things Sweet

All Things Sweet by Stormy, was founded 2 years ago by 12-year-old Stomry Colon. First she started entering her cupcakes in baking contests and then special gift baskets for classmates birthdays. She has now expanded to making all kinds of desserts such as cakes, pies, cookies and more. The orders starting pouring in so with the help of her family she has started All Things Sweet and now caters small events to large business meetings. Stormy's dedication to making children smile and still keep up with her schoolwork is the reason we've created this site so you can place orders and view monthly featured content 24/7. If Stormy or anyone else on her staff isn't available you can expect a response to your messages within 12 hours even on weekends !
